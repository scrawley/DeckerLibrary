What We Offer

Collections Use

Classes are invited to schedule research opportunities whether it's with a librarian or not -- to use the main collection or special collections. Because of the heavy use of the Digital Library and main areas of the Library, classes may only be scheduled if they are here to use the library collection for research. Drop-ins to do research on your own are welcome yet scheduling is advised so that we might reserve a space for you. To learn more please contact the Information Desk at 410-225-2273 (ext. 2273 on campus) or refer@mica.edu.

Reserves

Course-related Reserves are circulated from the Circulation Desk for in-house use. Materials can be copied or scanned on the lower level of the Library.

Digital Imaging

The Decker Library can assist faculty members with creating digital images for presentation and instruction use. We have the ability to scan from 35mm slide and a variety of print materials up to 12" x 15". Depending on the quantity of images to be digitized, please allow for a turnaround time of one to two weeks. Contact Media Coordinator Arthur Soontornsaratool or phone (410) 462-7521 for more information.

Course Evaluations

Recognizing that Faculty who teach courses in Bunting do not have access to a dedicated computer classroom, Decker Library is prepared to assist with this new process supporting electronic course evaluations.

Copy-Print-Scan

The Copy-Print-Scan equipment on the lower level of the Library requires Flex Account funds for copying and printing, while scanning is free provided the user brings a storage device to take their files. Complete information and directions appear on the link at left. See map below for location of facilities.

Subject Liaisons

Students can view a list of their checked-out items -- and renew items -- using the Library Catalog by clicking on "My Account". Items can be renewed just twice through the catalog; for further renewals, visit or call the Circulation Desk (410) 225-2272.

Screening Room

The Decker Library Screening Room is to be used by current MICA students, faculty, and staff for group screenings and study sessions only. Groups may book the room for up to three hours during Library hours. Exceptions will be made on a case by case basis. At the beginning of a group's designated time slot, the key to the screening room will be checked out to one member of the group at the library circulation desk. This individual will be responsible for returning the key and ensuring that the room and all equipment are in order. Loss of the key will result in a $50 fine. No food or uncovered beverages are allowed in the screening room. Headphones must be used during screenings. If a group does not show up within the first 30 minutes of the designated time slot, the screening room reservation may be cancelled and the room may be rebooked by others. For questions, comments, and concerns regarding the Screening Room please contact circ@mica.edu or 410-225-2272.

Computer Workstations

Computer workstations are available on both levels of the Library, both iMacs and PCs. The entire Library is wireless enabled. Laptop use is supported on the lower level of the library, with pull-down electrical outlets above the study tables and a Laptop Lounge with flexible seating.
